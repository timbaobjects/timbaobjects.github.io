Birth Registration

Birth Registration is a an application that was built for UNICEF Nigeria. Birth Registration application is used to monitor birth registration of children, and registration of underage children in Nigeria. Monitors are assigned to hospital centers in different locations all over the country, and they are required to send the number of registered child births, and registered underage children in each location two times in a month. Reports sent in are monitored on the Birth Registration web platform, and further analysis is carried out on reported data and performances. Birth Registration is built on RapidSMS. visit website
